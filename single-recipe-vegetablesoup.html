How to Make vegetable Soup
Want a meal with veggies? Here's a Vegetable soup that doesn’t have to be bland and boring. This recipe is so hearty and delicious, you’ll hardly realize it’s vegetarian. It’s a comforting, feel-good soup and tastes so much better than canned or store-bought.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1 : Aromatics

APreheat your soup pot with olive oil. Saute onions and carrots until golden.

step 2:Add Vegetables and Broth

Add celery, canned tomatoes, broth, potatoes, green beans, and bay leaves. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Simmer for 25 minutes.

step 3: Finishing Touches

Add corn, sweet peas, green onion, and parsley. Simmer for an additional 5-8 minutes then serve.
