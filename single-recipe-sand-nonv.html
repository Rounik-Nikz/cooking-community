Chicken Club Sandwich

How to Make a Chicken Club Sandwich
Club sandwich is a dish of American origin. It is also known as club house sandwich. It is basically a sandwich of bread(toasted) slice, cooked poultry or fried bacon, Lettuce, tomato and mayonnaise.s no sugar.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

6 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1 : Mix, Marinade & Chop

Roast the chicken breast with salt, olive oil and black pepper powder. You can boil it and mix salt and pepper later. Toast the breads in a bread toaster or on a pan using gas top method. Make a fried egg and apply salt and pepper powder. Slice the tomato into thin sized slices around 4 - 5 slices. Shredded the roasted chicken breast and mix with one teaspoon of mayonnaise.

step 2:Assemble

Take the bread slices and apply mayonnaise on one side of each bread. Now take one bread slice and arrange lettuce leaves over the mayonnaise spread side. Then arrange the tomato slices over the lettuce leaves. Add a small pinch of salt over them.e Place the shredded mayo chicken over the tomato slices and close with the second mayo spread bread. Keep the mayonnaise part over the chicken. Now apply mayonnaise over the top of the second bread and place the fried egg over it. Similarly close it with the third bread and press slightly to set all the ingredients inside them. Now cut the four sides of the sandwich and then cut again into triangular shaped wise into four parts and serve.

step 3: Serve

Non club sandwich is served with some fries. Generally French fries is served with it. Coleslaw is also served along with a non veg club sandwich.

ingredients

3 slices of bread ( white or brown)

1 chicken breast

1 fresh Tomato

Herbs & Spices

2 Eggs

2-3 teaspoon mayonnaise
